# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name
YouTube Transcriber

## Short Description (132 chars max)
One-click transcription for YouTube videos and Spotify podcasts. Runs locally — your data never leaves your machine.

## Detailed Description
YouTube Transcriber adds a side panel to Chrome that lets you transcribe YouTube videos and Spotify podcast episodes with one click.

How it works:
• Click the extension icon to open the side panel
• Navigate to any YouTube video or Spotify podcast episode
• Hit "Transcribe" — the transcript appears in your local app
• All processing happens on your machine using Whisper

Key features:
• One-click transcription from the browser
• Side panel stays open as you browse
• Queue multiple videos for batch transcription
• View recent transcripts without leaving your tab
• Supports YouTube videos, Shorts, and Spotify podcast episodes

This extension works with the open-source YouTube Transcriber app running locally on your machine. No cloud account needed — your audio and transcripts stay on your computer.

Requirements:
• YouTube Transcriber app running locally (github.com/lifesized/youtube-transcriber)
• ffmpeg and yt-dlp installed
• A Whisper-compatible transcription provider (Groq, OpenAI, or local Whisper)
